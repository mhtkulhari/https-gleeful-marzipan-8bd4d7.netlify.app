Summary of MI vs RR match

MI won the toss and opted to bat first. MI played with same team while RR replaced Riyan Parag with Yashasvi Jaiswal.

In powerplay, partnership of Ishan Kisan and Rohit Sharma helped MI to score 63/0 in 6 overs.

Due to a great bowling spell by Jofra Archer, RR managed to stop MI at a score of 195/4.

In second innings, MI bowlers showed brilliant performance but later on a fast fifty by Sanju Samson and some extra-ordinary shots by Miller helped RR to chase the target easily.
