Summary of CSK vs RCB match

CSK won the toss and opted to ball first. There were two new young faces for them in replacement of injured Deepak Chahar and unavailable Chris Jordan.

In powerplay, partnership of Padikkal and Kohli helped RCB to score 78/0 in 6 overs.

Due to a great bowling spell by DJ Bravo, CSK managed to stop RCB at a score of 184/7.

In second innings, RCB bowlers showed brilliant performance but later on a fast fifty by MS Dhoni and some extra-ordinary shots by Jadega helped CSK to chase the target easily.
